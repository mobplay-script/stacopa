Case Study

We take pride in our capabilities to provide solutions and to accurately deliver promises. Continuously enhancing satisfactory level through precise and customized handling of clients’ requests at any levels of difficulties.

KPU (General Election Commision of Indonesia)

Product

Ballot Paper

 

Challenge

  • Providing extreme-highly secure papers to diminish every single possibility of forgery, tampering, or counterfeiting.
  • Heavy time constraints due to rigorous content revisions resulting in pending productions
  • National scale distribution including to sub-district level offices through air and ground transport

 

Result

  • Extreme-highly secure papers with anti forgery/ tampering/ counterfeiting quality
  • Precise and timely production completion
  • Delivery precision even to the farthest sub-district offices and the furthest ends of Indonesia

Kantor Dinas Pendidikan (Office of Education Bureau)

Product

National Exam Papers

 

Challenge

  • Extremely sensitive information to be printed required a total production security with detailed and thorough monitoring
  • Short and strict time constrain
  • Multiple versions of exam papers designed to minimize risk of information leak and discourage any attempts of misconducts. This method added tremendous complexity toward the entire production management
  • Meticulous content that required precise packaging, presented difficulties in sorting and bundling processes
  • National scale distribution including the sub-district level offices through air and ground transport

 

Result

  • A total lockdown. No access and communication to-and-from the production plant to ensure information security.
  • Accurate and timely production completion
  • Coordination and cooperation with the National Police and multiple Governmental agencies to securely escort the final printed goods to its intended destinations.
  • Delivery precision even to the farthest sub-district offices and the furthest ends of Indonesia

Banks

Product

Cheques and Giro papers

 

Challenge

  • Reduce the running cost of the Banks’ Internal Cheques and Giros personalization Unit
  • To build a secure and efficient communication system between banks and Stacopa, ensuring delivery speed
  • Timely distribution to multiple bank branches

 

Result

  • Centralized Perso Service a fully personalized  Cheques and Giros production service
  • Simplified mechanism of customized Checks and Giro paper printing
  • Banks provide personalization data; we print the end products that are ready to be dispersed to any branches and locations as required
  • Entrusted by banks to carry such vital information
  • Diminish expensive cost in internal-customized printing
