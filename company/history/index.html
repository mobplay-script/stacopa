History

PT. Stacopa Raya initiated a Business form Solution in 1977. With utmost determination, strong network and high regard in product quality, the company obtained a Security Printer Certification in 1987 from Badan Intelijen Negara-Badan Koordinasi Pemberantasan Uang Palsu (the National Intelligence Agency – Anti Counterfeiting Coordinating Agency). Subsequently, PT. Stacopa Raya became one of the most trusted security printing services in the country with certifications from Bank Indonesia (Central Bank of Indonesia) and Kementrian Keuangan-Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (the Ministry of Finance-Directorate General of Taxes) in the same year of 1987.

To expand production capacity, the company set-up a 2nd production plant in 1990. Accommodating government official papers and printing products with highly regarded security and product quality output.

Over the course of 36 years in Security Printing Services, PT. Stacopa Raya has acquired multiple government certifications including ones from the Ministry of Forestry, Ministry of Transportation and gaining international recognitions including ISO 9001 Quality Certification, ISO 14001 Environment Management Certification, and strategic partnership with Arsegor Sdn BHD Malaysia to name a few.

1977

PT. Stacopa Raya was established in Jakarta as a Business Form Solution.

1987

PT. Stacopa Raya gained a Security Printer Certification from Badan Intelijen Negara-Badan Koordinasi Pemberantasan Uang Palsu (the National Intelligence Agency – Anti Counterfeiting Coordinating Agency Indonesia).

1987

The company acquired two more certifications from Bank Indonesia(Central Bank of Indonesia) and Kementrian Keuangan-Direktorat Jenderal Pajak (the Ministry of Finance-Directorate General of Taxes Indonesia).

1990

The 2nd production plant was built to expand the company’s production capacity.

2009

Received a certification from Kementrian Dalam Negeri (Ministry of Home Affairs of Indonesia).

2009

Received a certification from Kementrian Kehutanan (Ministry of Forestry of Indonesia).

2010

Combining 2 production centers into a unified factory to maximize control

2010

Obtained ISO 9001: 2008 that specifies management quality standard through numerous demonstrations of consistent service records and efficient management system.

2010

Obtained ISO 14001 that specifies a good standard in environmental management system.

2010

Established the Smart Card Production Line.

2011

Acquired Card Security Printing Certification from the National Intelligence Agency.

2012

Voted as One of the Best Printer in Indonesia by PPGI (Indonesia’s Printer Association).

2012

Acquired and distribute Sandilock, a personal data security system.

2013

Established strategic partnership with Arsegor Sdn BHD Malaysia to market Stacopa Products and distribute Sandilock in Malaysia.
