Clients

With more than 36 years of experience in security printing, PT. Stacopa Raya has managed to acquire trust and confidence from clients in multiple industries. Over achieving satisfactory level and supreme product quality are two of the main reasons that makes the company a National leader and a top preference for the most challenging cases.

Government

Government partnerships have been the “backbone” of PT. Stacopa Raya since the beginning. High and advanced professionalism even at the most critical times are testaments of the company’s quality over its years of service.
